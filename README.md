## Research

EEG microstates with variational autoencoders, at the AI and Cognitive Load Research Lab. The work learns explainable, disentangled latent structure over scalp topographic maps: probabilistic soft clustering in place of classical k-means. Core model is Conv-VaDE (Convolutional Variational Deep Embedding) with a Gaussian-mixture prior, trained on the LEMON resting-state dataset.

**Publications**

- **Interpretable EEG Microstate Discovery via Variational Deep Embedding: A Systematic Architecture Search with Multi-Quadrant Evaluation.** XAI 2026 (late-breaking + Doctoral Consortium), Fortaleza, Brazil, 1-3 July 2026. With Andrea Visentin and Luca Longo. Best GEV 0.730, silhouette 0.229 at K=4, depth L=4, across a 4,832-model sweep. [arXiv:2605.10947](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.10947) · [microstate-architecture-search](https://github.com/fayisode/microstate-architecture-search)
- **Explainable Disentangled Representation Learning of Recurring Brain Activation Patterns via Variational Autoencoders.** XAI World Conference 2025, Doctoral Proposals track.
